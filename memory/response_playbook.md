## 1. Pricing / Licensing Questions
**Trigger:** Asks about cost, per-seat, per-license, bulk pricing, quote, how licensing works
**Steven's pattern:** First preference is always to push for a Zoom call to discuss options, answer questions, and tailor a quote. If they push back on a call or after already trying, Steven will send pricing in email. For international leads/contacts, send pricing directly in the email without pushing for a call (unless it's a significant opportunity).
**Real snippets:**
- "Want to hop on a quick call so I can walk you through everything and get you a fresh quote?"
- "I can go over all the options and pricing on a call. Pick any time here: [calendar link]"
- "You have 3 options: the first two come with the teacher dashboard, progress tracking, and a plethora of teacher and student resources."
- "Our minimum purchase order is 10 annual licenses for $70 each ($700)."
- "For 20 licenses, the cost would be as follows. $50 x 20 licenses for 1 year = $1000."
**Typical length:** Medium. When pushing for a call, focus on what's INCLUDED (the value) and end with scheduling link. When sending pricing (after call attempt or international), use bullet lists and tables.
**Key rule:** Default to pushing for a Zoom call first. Send pricing in email only after trying for a call, if they push back, or for international leads. Calendar link: https://codecombat.orumbriel.com/c/steven

- Automated notifications (Outreach, Salesforce, PandaDoc, Dialpad, calendar)
- Newsletters, marketing emails, promotions
- CC-only threads where Steven isn't directly addressed
- Sales outreach or recruiting emails TO Steven
- Internal company-wide announcements (Slab, Expensify, etc.)
- Support ticket auto-responses
- Bid/procurement system notifications (ionwave, etc.)
- Zoom meeting invites sent to Fireflies
